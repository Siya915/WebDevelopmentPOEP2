Our Story

First Bridge Flame-Grilled started in 2021 with a mission to bring rich, smoky flame-grilled flavours to the community of Kgapane. We value freshness, hospitable service, and memorable gatherings.

We offer daily grills and kotas, and we also host events with catering options and added services like carwash while you dine.

Our Values

  • Community-focused
  • Great value & portions
  • Convenience & hospitality
